The Learning Process

Our Roadmap to Academic Excellence

  • Step One

    Book Your Free Consultation

    Contact us through our short form, and we'll schedule a free preliminary online consultation session where we can discuss your academic goals, the classes and topics you're looking for help in, and how we can plan for your future.

  • Step Two

    Schedule Your First Session

    After the consultation, we will pair you with your preferred specialist or with a tutor who we think is a good fit. We'll also create a personalized online lesson plan based on your academic goals and your learning style.

  • Step Three

    Transition to Full Capture

    Students frequently make the mistake of memorizing material, without fully capturing the concepts. We'll break down complicated topics for you into bite-sized ideas, and show you how to absorb information without needing to perform rote memorization.

  • Step Four

    Review, Rinse, and Repeat

    Once you've absorbed the material, we'll help you review the concepts so that we can prepare you for additional learning. Our specialists will assign optional practice questions, and we'll guide you each step of the way.
