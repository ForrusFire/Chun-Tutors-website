Chun Tutors hopes to inspire academic success through personalized lessons and mentorship opportunities.

Our Mission

Chun Tutors began with the simple idea that the classroom or the lecture hall is not enough for unlocking the potential of each student. The current education system forces students into a one-size-fits-all box that limits learning and prevents academic achievements. We aim to break down this barrier and replace it with a learning environment that is custom tailored towards the studying style of each student.

Our goal is to create a sustainable tutoring network that can adapt to the learning patterns of the individual and provide customized paths towards knowledge and personal growth.

Company Background

Chun Tutors was founded in 2020 by Sam.

In 2016, Sam started private tutoring students with their math and physics problem sets. Over the years, he recognized the different learning styles of his students and found that he could communicate with each student more effectively by creating personal lesson plans and by discussing their long-term academic goals. He also found that most students were simply going about their assignments ineffectively, and wasting far too much time on the wrong concepts.

Equipped with this knowledge, Sam assembled a reliable team of expert educators and professional tutors, who are experienced in guiding students with increasing their productivity and the quality of their work in both their academic and professional careers.

Reliability

Integrity

Community
